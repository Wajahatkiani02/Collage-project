Three year diploma of Architecture

What does an Architect do?

Architects design the house, offices, apartments, commercial, educational, recreational and other such buildings. An Architect also caters that the buildings should not only be safe & sound and provide a pleasing aesthetics but also be comfortable to the users. An Architect draws up the final plans showing how a building sahll lock after its completion and how it shall be built. He also looks into the services to be installed in a building along with its security checks, etc. He also capable to draw all these plans with the help of computer software. An Architect spend most of the time in office; however, he is supposed to visit the construction site of the building he design to see if it is being constructed as per his instruction.
