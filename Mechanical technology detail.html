Three year diploma in Mechanical

Mechanical engineering technology is the application of engineering principles and technological development to the creation of useful machinery or products. Software tools such as Finit Element Analysis (FEA) and / or Computational fluid dynamics (CFD) are often used to analyze part and assemblies.

What does a DAE Mechanical technologist do?

It is three year post matric program in Mechanical technology. Its deal with basic concept of Mechanical and the related equipment. No industry can run without Mechanical technologists whether it is process industry or manufacturing industry. In short we can say that wheel of industry and wheel of progress & prosperity is almost run by Mechanical technologists.
