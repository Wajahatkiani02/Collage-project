Library

Askari college possess a well established technical library with a quite large number of volume related to technology and Engineering. The institute is in a process of getting access to the E-library established by H.E.C and A.I.O.U.

Book bank

A book bank has also been established to lend technical books to the students. These books are also sold out to the students after the 2nd issue. The bank also helps students in purchasing/selling of technical books to the students which are not available locally.

Hostel

An excellence hostel facility is available to students coming from far off areas. We may accommadate more than 100 students.

Play Ground

Play ground play a significant role in sports activities. Foot Ball, Cricket, Hockey, Athletics track, Badminton, Basketball, and Volly Ball court have been prepared for students practice as well as for internal and extrnal tournaments.

Fool Proof Security

Askari college possess best fool proof Security system at its end with latest weopeons, gadgets and walk through gates. Security cameras are covering the whole area and are 24/7 under observation. Security guards are vigilant and alert enough to cater any situation which may appear threstening.

Admission

Minimum qualification

Secondary school Certificate (SSC) in science group from any board of intermediate & Secondary Education with the subjects of Mathematics, English, Chemistry and physics as a whole as well as the mentioned subjects individualy.

  • Minimum 50% marks in matriculation as a whole is essential.

Fee Instruction

The candidate shall only be on roll if he deposite the fee as per Instructions.


  • If a candidate fails to remit the fee up to the specific date, his right to get admission is forteited and admission to the next applicant on merit shall be offered. No representation shall be accepted at this stage.

  • Fee shall be recieved in advance monthly

  • A fine of Rs.50 per day shall be levied if a student fails ot deposite his/her fee up to 15 days beyond the notified date for the subsequent month.

  • If one does not care to deposite even after this limit, his/her admission shall be cancelled. The re-admission shall only possible after depositing the re-admission fee and due fine

Fee strucure

Admission fee Rs. 5000/- (One time)
Security fee Rs. 5000/- (Refundable)
Tution fee Rs. 3570/month
PBTE Registration fee Rs. 1500/- (One time)
Exam fee Rs. 1000/- (One time)
Total Dues Rs. 16,000 (At the time of admission)
Hostel Charges Rs. 7500
Hostel Security Rs. 5000 (Refundable)

Note

The deposite dates are final unless changed & notified by administration.

Fee concession policies

a. Fee education scholarship (full tution fee concession) for the success year to the students who achieve above 85% marks in board exam and 50% concession in monthly fee to the students who achieve marks between 80% to 85% in board exam.

b. 100% Fee concession in monthly fee to the wards of Shaheeds.

c. Fee concession @ 50% in monthly fee to the civ Orphan students, subject to scoring 70% marks or above in annual exam.

d. 50% Fee concession in monthly fee to the wards of Askari college Emplyees.

e. 50% fee concession in monthly fee to the subsequent child.

f. 10% fee concession in monthly fee the wards of Serving/Retired Armed Forces Personals and serving Emplyees of Defence Organizations.

Attendence

  • It is comulsory for students to attend at least 80% lectures and practical, otherwise they will not be able to appear in the college/Board examination.

  • A fine of Rs. 50/- per day shall be levied if the student is absent from his class.

  • Absent in a single period shall be threated as absent for the whole day. The fine shall be collected along with fee.
