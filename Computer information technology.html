What does an IT engineer do?

Information technology has become a major driving force in many organizatins. this technology has solved many problems of people. it has been use in health, educaton, agriculture as well as many other sector of social life. Information technology deals and has jobs related to electronic and software of computer to store, convert, process, protect, retrieve securly and transmit information. In recent days this technology and the professionals in this field have encompassed every espect of technology and computing. it has achieved more recognition today than in the past. The IT professionals as they are refferd as perform varius duties, which rang from the application of installation to even designing some computer networks of a complex nature. Along with these, some database applications are a part of their job profile. Some also perform management of data, engineering, networking, design of software and database, then engineering of hardware and even system administration and management too.
