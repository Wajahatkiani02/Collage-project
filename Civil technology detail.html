What does a DAE civil technologist do?

An associate engineer is a helping hand to engineers. He not only supervisos the construction sites, but also perform as a liaison between a design engineer and the constructors, mason and other workers, involved directly in construction works. He also observes that the constructors should adobt the laid down specifications and procedure for that particular construction site. This way he directly supervises the work of mason and other skilled workers. He, also, supervises site of construction like buildings, road, public health schemes, concrete work, and material testing for sites, supervises labour and handle minor site problems during his duties. Sometimes, he is also entrusted to work as Quantity Surveyor or a QC/QA technologist.
